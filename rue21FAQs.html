Frequently Asked Questions

Am I able to shop online?

Yes, we now support online shopping, which includes shipping, on our unofficial page.

Is there a return policy?

Yes, you are able to return clothes up to a month after purchasing if unworn. If worn the warranty is voided.

Can i pay with card?

We do not take payments with card online, but support other methods such as paypal. Please come to one of our stores in person in order to pay with card, thanks for your understanding!

Is my data safe?

Of course! We do not permanently save your information in our servers, only harmless public information. If you wish to learn more about our privacy policies, visit our Privacy Policy page below!
